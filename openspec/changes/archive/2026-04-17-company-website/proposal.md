## Why

As a One-Person Company (OPC) focusing on high-value services, establishing a professional online presence is critical for building trust and showcasing expertise. This website will clearly communicate the two core business offerings: technical consulting/co-running for traditional startups, and Agent construction/enterprise digital transformation.

## What Changes

- Create a brand new static website using Hugo to serve as the OPC's primary landing page.
- Add a homepage highlighting the OPC's value proposition.
- Add a services section covering:
  - Technical consulting & co-running for traditional startups.
  - Agent construction and enterprise digital transformation.
- Add an "About" or "Contact" section for lead generation.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `opc-landing-page`: The core landing page layout showcasing the dual business model.
- `service-descriptions`: Detailed pages/sections explaining technical co-running and Agent generation services.
- `contact-mechanism`: A way for potential clients to reach out (e.g., mailto links, contact form, or social links).

### Modified Capabilities
- (None, this is a new project)

## Impact

- A new Hugo site will be initialized in the workspace or a designated subdirectory.
- The project will introduce a Hugo theme and custom content to reflect the business services.
