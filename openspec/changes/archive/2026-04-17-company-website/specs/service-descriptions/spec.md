## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Service Descriptions
The site SHALL outline two specific services:
1) Co-running technical output for traditional start-ups.
2) Agent construction and enterprise digital transformation.

#### Scenario: User visits technical co-running service page
- **WHEN** a user clicks on the "Technical Consulting" service
- **THEN** they are presented with a detailed page on how the OPC helps traditional startups.

#### Scenario: User visits Agent building service page
- **WHEN** a user clicks on the "Agent Construction" service
- **THEN** they are presented with details on AI agents and digital transformation.
