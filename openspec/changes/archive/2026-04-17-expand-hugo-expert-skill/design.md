## Context
The current `hugo-expert` skill covers only basic site setup. Users need assistance with advanced Hugo features like Templates, Functions, Variables, Hugo Modules, Hugo Pipes, Developer Tools, and Deployment, as seen in the broader Hugo documentation sidebar.

## Goals / Non-Goals

**Goals:**
- Expand the knowledge base in `SKILL.md` for `hugo-expert` to cover advanced topics.
- Provide clear instructions to the AI on how to handle complex Hugo workflows.

**Non-Goals:**
- We are not creating a new Hugo site here, only expanding the skill's capabilities.

## Decisions
- Add specific bullet points and sections in `SKILL.md` for the missing advanced topics:
  - **Templates & Views** (模板, 方法, 函数)
  - **Hugo Modules & Pipes** (模块, 管道)
  - **Troubleshooting & Development** (故障排除, 开发者工具, 命令行进阶)
  - **Hosting & Deployment** (托管和部署)
- Instruct the AI to act as a deep technical advisor on these topics.

## Risks / Trade-offs
- Risk: The prompt may become longer.
- Trade-off: It's worth it for the improved capability to assist users with real-world Hugo projects that require advanced pipelines and templates.
